In the next corollary we obtain the sharp  $S^{*}(\beta)$ - radius for the class  $\mathcal{F}_{\mathcal{L}\mathcal{P}}$ .

Corollary 2.12. Suppose  $0\leq \beta < 1$  and  $f\in \mathcal{F}_{\mathcal{L}\mathcal{P}}$ , then sharp  $S^{*}(\beta)$ - radius is  $\tanh^2 (\pi \sqrt{\beta} /2\sqrt{2})$ .

Remark 2.13. On replacing  $\beta$  with  $1 - \alpha$  ( $0< \alpha \leq 1$ ), in Corollary 2.12 we get the radius of starli kness of order  $\alpha$  obtained in Corollary 2.8. Moreover, from Corollary 2.12 we obtain the sharp  $S_{\alpha}^{*}$ - radius for the class  $\mathcal{F}_{\mathcal{L}\mathcal{P}}$ , where  $S_{\alpha}^{*} = \{f\in \mathcal{A}:|zf'(z) / f(z) - 1|< 1 - \alpha \}$ .
