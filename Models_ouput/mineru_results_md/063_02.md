Let $p$ be an odd prime and denote by $\mathbb { F } _ { p } = \mathbb { Z } / p \mathbb { Z }$ a finite field of $p$ elements. Recall that a codeword of the form $\{ 0 , a , 2 a \}$ is said to be equi-difference. In the paper [LTo5], the authors show that there exists an optimal CAC consisting of $\textstyle { \frac { p - 1 } { 4 } }$ equi-difference codewords in the case where $4 \mid o _ { p } ( 2 )$ .
